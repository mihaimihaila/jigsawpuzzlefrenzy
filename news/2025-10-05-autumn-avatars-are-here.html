Autumn Avatars Are Here!

Mihai Mihaila, 05/10/2025

We’re welcoming the season with a fresh set of autumn-themed avatars—designed to bring cozy vibes and a splash of seasonal charm to your puzzle profile.

Whether you’re solving solo or sharing your progress with friends, these avatars add a warm, festive touch to your in-game identity. From crisp leaves to comfy sweaters, each design reflects the spirit of fall and pairs perfectly with our seasonal puzzle packs.

To update your avatar, simply head to the profile settings in-game and select your favorite autumn look. It’s quick, easy, and a great way to personalize your puzzling experience.
