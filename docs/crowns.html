Crowns

Author: Mihai, last modified: 04/01/2024


Crowns are used for unlocking puzzles.

Earning Crowns

Crowns are earned by:

  • Creating a game account
  • Completing missions
  • Recommendeding the game to a friend

How to get Crowns

  • You can purchase crowns from the Bank
  • You convert coins into crowns at the Market
