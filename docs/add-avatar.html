Add Avatar

Author: Mihai, last modified: 15/12/2020


Make your profile stand out by picking an avatar that represents you.

To add an avatar follow these steps:

  1. Go to your game account
  2. Edit your account (tap on the pencil button next to your name)
  3. Select an avatar
  4. Tap on Save
