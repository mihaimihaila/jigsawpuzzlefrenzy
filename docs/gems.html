Gems

Author: Mihai, last modified: 04/01/2024


Gems are used for:

  • Playing Puzzle of the Day
  • Skipping Ads
  • Refilling tools: Shuffle, Peek, Move to right, Move to left, Edges only

How to get Gems

  • You can purchase gems from the Bank
  • You convert coins into gems at the Market
