Reset Password

Author: Mihai, last modified: 08/12/2024


Follow these steps to reset your password

  1. Navigate to the home page then tap on the account button (top right corner), then on See my account

2. Tap on Sign in (top center section)

3. Tap on Sign in (Existing player section)

4. Tap on Forgot password

5. Enter your email address then tap Find

6. Tap on Reset password

7. Enter the new password you received over email then tap on Continue

8. You are now signed in!
